Our Services

Insight is a website that utilizes maps and locations to inform users about esential COVID-19 statistics & information at specific locations. We created a map that highlights information regarding COVID-19 throughout the whole USA. We also created statistics for other countries if you are interested to see the data. We even provide updated news concerning important information related to Covid-19 and tips to help prevent the spread of Covid-19. Lastly,we know that many people are very paranoid about their exposures with COVID-19, so we created a free quiz that can calculate how likely you are to get contracted with the virus. We hope you getter a deeper dive into the world of COVID-19 using our website!
